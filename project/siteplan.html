Product Review - UAI Store

The name UAI comes from a popular regional expression in Minas Gerais, Brazil. It is often used to show surprise or curiosity (similar to saying “huh?” or “wow?”). I chose it because it represents local identity and feels familiar to people in Minas—perfect for a store focused on serving this region.

Purpose

Provide affordable clothing, home accessories, and personal items with delivery across Minas Gerais, Brazil. The store will highlight daily discounts, make product browsing easy with filters, and offer clear customer support contact options.

Scenarios

1) Which products have the best discounts today?
Products on sale will display a discount badge. Visitors can also use filters (for example, “Highest discount”) to quickly find the best deals of the day.
2) Where can I get help with my purchase?
The site will include contact information in the header and on a support area, so customers can ask questions, report issues, and get help with orders.
